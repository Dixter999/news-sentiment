# Historical Data Collection

Collect historical economic and sentiment data for PPO/SAC trading model training.

## Goals

- Build comprehensive training dataset for reinforcement learning models
- Cover 3+ years of historical economic events
- Enable backtesting of sentiment-based trading strategies

## Scope

- ForexFactory economic calendar backfill (2022-2024)
- Historical sentiment analysis
- Data quality validation
- Export formats for ML training

## Success Criteria

- 15,000+ economic events collected
- Full coverage of Jan 2022 - Dec 2024
- Data validated and cleaned
- Ready for PPO/SAC model training
